SECTION 01
Executive Summary
This proposal outlines the terms of engagement between TitanAI LLC ("Developer") and
Andrew Collazo ("Contributor") regarding the Sportsphere sports community platform.
TitanAI LLC has developed Sportsphere from an initial concept into a fully functional,
production-grade application comprising 69 pages, 205 components, 50 database tables,
and a comprehensive AI-powered feature suite. The platform is live, deployed, and tested
with a 98.1% automated test pass rate.
This document defines the scope of work completed, its market value, the respective
contributions of each party, and the proposed terms for an ongoing milestone-based equity
partnership.
Key Principle: TitanAI LLC retains 100% ownership of all intellectual property, code, and
infrastructure. Andrew Collazo receives 35% equity upon signing, reflecting his concept,
network, and commitment to the Front of House role. He can earn an additional 15% (to 50%)
through measurable business milestones.
