Section 01

Executive Summary

This proposal outlines the terms of engagement between TitanAI LLC ("Developer") and Andrew Collazo ("Contributor") regarding the Sportsphere sports community platform.

TitanAI LLC has developed Sportsphere from an initial concept into a fully functional, production-grade application comprising 69 pages, 205 components, 50 database tables, and a comprehensive AI-powered feature suite. The platform is live, deployed, and tested with a 98.1% automated test pass rate.

This document defines the scope of work completed, its market value, the respective contributions of each party, and the proposed terms for an ongoing milestone-based equity partnership.

Key Principle: TitanAI LLC retains 100% ownership of all intellectual property, code, and infrastructure. Andrew Collazo receives 25% equity upon signing, reflecting his initial concept and network access. He can earn an additional 20% (to 45%) through measurable business milestones with defined deadlines. TitanAI’s equity is protected from dilution — any investor equity comes from Andrew’s pool first.
